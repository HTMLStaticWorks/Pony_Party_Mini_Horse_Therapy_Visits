Healing Through Horses

How Therapy Visits Work

Our certified therapy ponies provide proven emotional, sensory, and physical benefits in a safe, supervised environment.

Initial Consultation

We discuss your needs, the recipient's background, sensory sensitivities, and goals for the visit. All at no charge.

Pony Matching

We select the best-matched certified therapy pony based on temperament, size, and the client's specific therapeutic needs.

The Visit

A licensed handler and certified therapy pony arrive at your location. Sessions include petting, grooming, leading, and bonding time.

Follow-Up & Scheduling

We provide post-visit notes and recommendations. Regular sessions can be scheduled for ongoing therapeutic benefit.

Site Requirements

Paddock & Venue Requirements

To ensure the safety of our ponies and your guests, here's what we need at your location.

Minimum Space

At least 20×20 ft of flat, open outdoor or covered space per pony.

Fencing or Barriers

Temporary fencing can be provided. Existing secure barriers are also acceptable.

Fresh Water Access

Access to fresh water within 50ft. We bring our own buckets and feed.

Vehicle Access

Clear access road for our trailer (minimum 10ft wide, no low branches).

Pet Safety

Other pets must be secured away from pony area during the visit.

Surface Type

Grass, dirt, or rubber matting preferred. No asphalt or concrete for extended standing.
