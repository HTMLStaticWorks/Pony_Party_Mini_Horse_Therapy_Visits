Healing Through Horses

The Therapeutic Magic of Our Herd

Our certified therapy ponies provide more than just a visit — they offer a pathway to emotional, sensory, and physical well-being.

Emotional Support

Developing a bond with a mini horse helps reduce anxiety, builds empathy, and provides unconditional emotional support for children and adults alike.

  • Reduces Cortisol Levels
  • Builds Self-Confidence

Sensory Processing

The tactile experience of grooming and touching a pony's soft coat provides vital sensory input, helping to calm the nervous system.

  • Tactile Stimulation
  • Calming Rhythmic Motion

Physical Magic

Leading a pony and participating in grooming activities encourages fine and gross motor skill development in a fun, non-clinical setting.

  • Hand-Eye Coordination
  • Active Movement

Calm & Focus

Interaction with our gentle herd encourages mindfulness and presence, helping individuals stay grounded and in the moment.

  • Mindfulness Training
  • Stress Reduction

Site Requirements

Paddock & Venue Requirements

To ensure the safety of our ponies and your guests, here's what we need at your location.

Minimum Space

At least 20×20 ft of flat, open outdoor or covered space per pony.

Fencing or Barriers

Temporary fencing can be provided. Existing secure barriers are also acceptable.

Fresh Water Access

Access to fresh water within 50ft. We bring our own buckets and feed.

Vehicle Access

Clear access road for our trailer (minimum 10ft wide, no low branches).

Pet Safety

Other pets must be secured away from pony area during the visit.

Surface Type

Grass, dirt, or rubber matting preferred. No asphalt or concrete for extended standing.

Not sure if your venue qualifies? We'll do a free site assessment!
