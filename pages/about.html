Our Story

From a backyard dream to a full-service pony experience company — we've been spreading magic since 2018.

Our Story

Born from a Love of Horses

It all started when founder Emma Rose brought her mini horse, Daisy, to her niece's birthday party in 2018. The joy on every child's face was indescribable — and the idea for Pony Party was born that afternoon.

Today, we operate a certified herd of 12 carefully trained ponies, a team of passionate handlers, and a mission to bring healing and happiness to every event we attend. From birthday parties to hospital therapy visits, we believe every moment deserves a little magic.
